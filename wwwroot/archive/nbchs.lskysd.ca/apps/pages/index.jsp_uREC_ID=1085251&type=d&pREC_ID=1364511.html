May Scholarships

​Please note that the scholarships highlighted in green were open to students during the 2019-20 year.  As they become available again this year, they will appear in blue and black font. 

 

 

Above and Beyond Award

Deadline:  May 31, 2021

A $250 award for a SRC member.  Nominees are chosen by SRC Advisors.  The recipient will be a student who has demonstrated extra commitment to SRC sponsored activities.  Please include a paragraph on your application that outlines why you would be an excellent candidate for this award.  Applications are available at the SSO.

 

 

Alicia Hanwell Memorial Scholarship

Deadline:  May 14, 2021

A $1,000 scholarship for a FEMALE pursuing a career in STEM.  This scholarship is in memory of Alicia, who was very involved in the school and the community.  Her passion was computer science, and the family wishes to support someone who shares this love of the sciences.  Applications are available at the SSO.

 

 

AMGITS Community Involvement Association Award

Deadline:  May 21, 2020

$250 awarded to a student who is involved in the community and is entering post-secondary in any health-related field.  Applications are available at the SSO.    

 

 

Battlefords Realtors Award

Deadline:  May 31, 2019

A $500 award to a student who is a relative of a Battlefords realtor.  If no such candidate apply, then the recipient will be a student pursuing a post-secondary education in some aspect of business.  Academics, attendance, and school or community involvement will be taken into consideration.  Applications are available at the SSO. 

 

 

Battleford Furniture Entrepreneurship Award

Deadline:  (based on academics and determined by the school - no need to apply)

$350 award for the top Entrepreneurship graduate. 

 

 

Battleford Home Hardware Building Center Drama Award

Deadline:  (based drama performance/academics and determined by the school - no need to apply)

A $300 award to the top graduating Drama student.

 

 

Battleford's Agency Tribal Chiefs Award

Deadline:  May 31, 2021

A $500 award for a graduate displaying outstanding attendance and academic excellence who is pursuing post-secondary studies and who is a registered member of a BATC First Nation.  If no applicants are from BATC, then the award may be awarded to a registered member of any First Nation.  Visit the SSO for an application.

 

 

Battlefords Agricultural Society Award

Deadline:  May 22, 2019           

A $500 award for a student entering the College of Agriculture or pursuing a related agriculture post-secondary diploma/degree. Application forms are available at the SSO.

 

 

Battlefords Boys and Girls Club - Mary Friedman Memorial Scholarship

Deadline:  May 6, 2019

$500, available to any past or present staff/member of The Boys and Girls Club.  A 500-word essay is required.  For more information and to apply, visit the website.

 

 

Battlefords Community Players Award

Deadline:  May 22, 2019

A $100 award for a graduate who has volunteered with BCP.  If not applicable, the award goes to a graduate who plans to attend post-secondary and excels in extracurricular drama.  Application forms are available at the SSO.

 

 

Battleford River Treaty 6 Health Centre Inc. Award                        

Deadline: May 1, 2020

You must be from a BTC community and be entering a post-secondary institute in a health-related field.  Good attendance and academic standing (70%) will be considered.  The award is for $300.  Applications forms are available at the SSO.

 

 

Battlefords and District Community Foundation Inc. Supervolley Tournament Education Fund

Deadline: (local - based on volleyball participation and determined by the school - no need to apply)

$608 for a graduating senior volleyball player who is entering post-secondary in the fall.

 

 

Battlefords Dance Festival Scholarship

Deadline:  May 31, 2019

$1,000 will be granted to a grade 12 student who is registered for a minimum of 3 hours per week in any ballet, tap, and jazz school in the Battlefords.  He/She must be pursuing post-secondary education in a program of eight months or longer.  IT DOES NOT HAVE TO BE A DANCE PROGRAM, as long as they are attending a post-secondary institute.  An essay explaining your involvement in school, extra-curricular activities, a brief description of the post-secondary/dance program and the reason for taking this program, as well as a reference letter must be attached.  Application forms are available in the SSO but must be returned to the address indicated on the form.

 

 

Battlefords Indian Metis Friendship Centre Award

Deadline: (local - based on academics and determined by the school - no need to apply)

A $250 award for an indigenous student excelling in Native Studies is available. 

 

 

Battlefords Union Hospital Healthcare Foundation

Deadline: May 8, 2020

$500 for a student entering the healthcare field.  He/She should also intend to be employed at BUH for at least one year of full-time work following the completion of their courses.  Letters, references and transcripts must be included in the application.  For more information and to apply, visit the SSO.

Deadline:  May 13, 2019 (so they can be delivered to the organization by the actual due date of May 15th)
