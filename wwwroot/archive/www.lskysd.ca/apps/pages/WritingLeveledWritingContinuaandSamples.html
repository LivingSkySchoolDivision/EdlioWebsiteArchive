Writing - Levelled Writing Continua and Samples

​​​​​​​​Back to Literacy

​Please note these files contain the new skills introduced at each level within the categories of Purpose, Genre and Craft, Conventions, and Write about Reading in the Fountas and Pinnell Literacy Continuum from Levels 1 to 8. 

The levels align with the Fountas and Pinnell text level gradient as follows: 

Level 1:  D to J, Level 2: J to M, Level 3: M to P, Level 4: P to S, Level 5: S to V, Level 6: V to Y, and Levels & and 8: Y and Z. 

Please click on each of the titles to expand the levels. Each level should have the file containing skills with the other files being examples for that level.
