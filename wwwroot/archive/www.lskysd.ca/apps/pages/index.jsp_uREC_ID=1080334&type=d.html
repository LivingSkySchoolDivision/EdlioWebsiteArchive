Schools

 
Living Sky School Division's 29 schools are located in 18 communities. Our schools include PreK-12, elementary, high schools, and alternate schools with a student population of 5,700 students. Our diverse school population is reflected in a wide variety of programs that exist throughout the schools to meet the guidelines of the Saskatchewan Core Curriculum and local needs of the community.
