Treaty Outcomes

"There must be an appreciation in the minds of the general public that Treaties are living, breathing documents that continue to bind us to promises made generations ago."
- Throne Speech, 2007​​

 
In 2013, the Ministry of Education released Treaty Outcomes and Indicators for K - 12. This was created in partnership with the Office of the Treaty Commissioner, teachers, consultants and elders.
 
In 2015, a Support Document to help instruction of the Treaty Education Outcomes and Indicators became available to Saskatchewan schools. It was created with pilot teachers across the province and features a cross-curricular approach to outcomes in Treaty Education and core subjects.
