CUPE SCHOLARSHIP
 
CUPE 4747 SCHOLARSHIP CRITERIA
 
1. Student must have a parent/guardian be a permanent member of CUPE Local 4747 for at least one full year.
2. Students must submit their application to CUPE Local 4747 including their high school marks and a short essay about the importance of a union to its members. It must be postmarked no later than April 20 of their graduating year.
3. Proof of attending a post-secondary school must be provided before the scholarship is paid out the following January by submitting a copy of your midterm or first year marks to the address below.
4. The CUPE 4747 Scholarship may be deferred for one year if the applicant is on a waiting list or not sure if attending a post-secondary school. A letter must be submitted to the scholarship committee with intent to be put on the waiting list.
5. Scholarship amount – $300.00.
6. When possible, a certificate will be given at the student’s graduation or awards ceremonies. The scholarship will be paid out directly to the student in January after receiving their postsecondary school progress marks.
7. Selection will be made by the CUPE Local 4747 executive.

LSky Educational Enhancement Award Application


The Living Sky School Division Educational Enhancement Award is available to grade 7-12 students of any ability level who wish to access an educational opportunity during the summer in an area at which they excel.  Special needs students with exceptional talents for students of like ability are also eligible. 

Eligible educational opportunities may include activities such as the fine arts, sports, academic pursuits, and civic studies.  In the past, students have received bursaries to attend summer camps focusing on one of the areas mentioned above.

Award amounts will be determined based upon applications received and funds available.  The application must be completed by all applicants and submitted to Luseland School by June 5th, 2018.  Applications will then be forwarded from our school to the division office.
