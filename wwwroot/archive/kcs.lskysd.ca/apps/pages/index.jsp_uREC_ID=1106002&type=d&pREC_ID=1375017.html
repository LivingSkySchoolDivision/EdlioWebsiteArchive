May Scholarships

​Bayer Fund Opportunity Scholarship

Deadline:  May 31, 2020

If you are enrolling in a post-secondary agriculture, food science or culinary program, you may be eligible for this $1,500 scholarship.  For an application, visit the SSO.

 

CWB Welding

Deadline: Varies between March and July

If you are interested in pursuing a career in welding, visit the webpage to learn about their scholarships.  Values and due dates vary.  Apply online.

 

Gabriel Dumont Foundation Scholarships

Deadline: May 1, 2020      

A variety of scholarships are available.  Apply online.

 

Innovation Credit Union

Deadline:  May 31, 2020

13 x $1,000 scholarships to students that are members of Innovation Credit Union (or their parents/guardians are members). For details and to apply, visit their webpage.

 

Les Donnelly Scholarship

Deadline: May 31, 2019

A $2,000 (AMOUNT NOT SPECIFIED) scholarship based on responses to questions regarding injury prevention initiatives/activities, financial need, extra-curricular activities and academic average.  Must provide proof of enrollment in college or university.  Applications available online.

 

 

Living Sky School Division #202 Board of Education Scholarship

Deadline:  (local - based on academics and determined by the school - no need to apply)

Three $500 scholarships for students pursuing post-secondary schooling.  Selection based on academic average. 

 

 

Loblaw Scholarship Fund

Deadline:  May 4, 2020

200 scholarships of $1,500 are available to students who are planning on continuing their education at a post-secondary institute.  Visit the website for more information and to apply.

 

 

Orville Erickson Memorial Scholarship  - Canadian Wildlife Federation   

Deadline: May 25, 2019

Scholarships range from $500 - $4,000.  You must be pursuing higher education in the field of conservation.  For details and to apply, visit their webpage.

 

SK Association of Student Council Advisors (SASCA)

Deadline: May 31, 2020

There are 12 - $500 awards available.  These awards are available to all SK students who are now in their final year of high school in a SASCA member school (NBCHS included).  It is based on school and community involvement and requires an essay and letters.  Visit the website for more information and to apply.

 

 

Saskatchewan Career Work Education Association Award

Deadline:  May 1, 2020

You DO NOT have to be going to post-secondary school to apply for this $200 award.  The only requirement is that you are graduating and currently enrolled (or have been enrolled) in a CWEX course.  You must also be on track to pass the course.  See Miss Merkowsky for further detail. 

 

 

Saskatchewan Curling Association

Deadline:  May 31, 2020

Several scholarships available, must be a member in good standing of an SCA affiliated curling club.  Candidate, must be actively curling the same year they win the award.

 

 

 

SK Drama Association Scholarship

Deadline:  May 31, 2020

An amount of $1,000 will be given to a student continuing education at a post-secondary institution with a major area of study in theatre/drama or theatre/drama education program.  You must have a 75% average and be a member of the SK Drama Association.  See the website for more details.

 

 

Saskatchewan Institute of Agrologists

Deadline: May 15, 2020 

6 scholarships of $1,000 for students who pursue careers in agriculture, bio resources, food or the environment.  Visit the website for more information and to learn about additional requirements (resume, essay, etc.).  Preference will be given to a University of Saskatchewan student.

 

 

Saskatchewan Pulse Growers

Deadline: May 4, 2019

SPG has 5 scholarships for undergraduate students enrolled in the fall in a Saskatchewan-based post-secondary agriculture program. To be eligible for one of these scholarships, the student or the student's parent(s) must be an active pulse producer in Saskatchewan (i.e. he/she has sold a Saskatchewan-grown pulse crop in the last two crop years).  Students must have a 70% minimum, be accepted in a full time Saskatchewan post-secondary program associated with the pulse industry, submit a transcript and write a 500-word essay.  For more information and to apply, visit their website.

 

 

 

Spirit of Sandra Schmirler Scholarship

Deadline: May 9, 2019

There are 6 x $5,000 scholarships for students who are curling in their graduating year and planning to attend a post-secondary institution. Students will also participate in a 2-day camp to hone their curling skills.  You must submit a video and 2 letters of recommendation. Please visit website for additional application information.

 

 

TransCanada Community Scholarships

Deadline: May 15, 2020

Three categories of scholarship available:  Community Leaders, Indigenous Legacy and Trades with a total of 80 scholarships to be awarded ranging in value between $1,000 and $5,000.  Check out the website for details and to apply online.
