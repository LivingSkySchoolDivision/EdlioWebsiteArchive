March

Agriculture Student Scholarship

Deadline:  March 1, 2021

Want a chance at winning either $4,000 or $2,000?  If you are planning on taking a program related to Agriculture at a post-secondary institute next year, then check out the website for more details and for the application form.  Be sure to read the fine print, as you will need either a video or an essay.

 

Canadian Association of Principals Student Leadership Award

Deadline: March 1, 2021

12 x $250, based on outstanding leadership in the school and community, academic achievement, and recognition of other awards.  Local school nominates one candidate, so let SSO know if you are interested and visit the website for application form.

 

CAA Saskatchewan Entrance Scholarship

Deadline:  In March – date varies depending on institute attending

A $2,500 scholarship is available to the University of Saskatchewan and the University of Regina. Applicants must be either a CAA Saskatchewan Member or a child of a Member.

$5,000 is available to Saskatchewan Polytechnic with the breakdown as follows: $2,500 is allocated between the Automotive Service Technician programs in Moose Jaw (Palliser Campus) and Saskatoon (Kelsey Campus) and $2,500 is allocated for the Auto Body Technician program in Regina (Wascana Campus). The CAA scholarships at Saskatchewan Polytechnic are available to CAA Saskatchewan Members and non-members.

 

Craig Hartl Memorial Scholarship (Goalie)

Deadline:  March 1 Each Year

A $1,000 scholarship in memory of Craig Hartl for a student entering a post-secondary institute that was registered in Saskatchewan as a goalie for at least three years.  For more information and to apply, visit the website.

 

Girl Guides Canada

Deadline: Over 10 National Scholarships - Opens February of Current Year

Must be a member of Girl Guides Canada and continue this membership in the upcoming year.

 

 

Innovation Credit Union

Deadline:  Not yet open - previously due March 27th

13 x $1,000 scholarships to students that are members of Innovation Credit Union (or their parents/guardians are members). For details and to apply, visit their webpage. 

 

Leonard Foundation

Deadline:  March 15 Each Year               

For students pursuing a degree in Education or Divinity, there are 140 awards of $1,000 to $1,500 available with preference given to a son/daughter of clergy, teacher, military, engineer or mining.  You must also plan on obtaining employment during your free time to help defray the costs and regularly participate in athletic, fitness or militia activities.

 

Prince of Wales and Duchess of Cornwall Scholarships

FOR GRADE 11 STUDENTS (click here)

Deadline: School deadline - March 6 - actual deadline for scholarship committee - March 15, 2021 

20 x $500, for grade 11 students demonstrating leadership, financial need, academic performance, and responsibilities outside of school.  Applications online.  Please note that you need a letter of recommendation. 

 

Queen's Diamond Jubilee Scholarship (see the SSO if interested)

Deadline: School deadline - March 1 - actual deadline for scholarship committee - March 15, 2021 

$1000 for demonstration of leadership, financial need, responsibilities outside of school and academic performance. This is a nominated scholarship, meaning you MUST request nomination from the SSO and then complete this form.

 

Remax Quest for Excellence Bursary

Deadline March 15, 2021   

16 x $1000 and 1 flight for two in the Re/Max Hot Air Balloon. Write an essay which conveys your motivation, leadership and communication in connection with your community efforts. Online applications only.  Applications open in September.

 

SaskTel Scholarships

Deadline:  March 31, 2021

7 scholarships of $3,000 will be awarded to students attending post-secondary programs (degree or certificate) related to telecommunications (for example; computer science, electrical engineering, computer software engineer, accounting, business administration).  Minimum average of 75% required.  Additional scholarship opportunities listed on their website.

 

Saskatchewan Agriculture Student Scholarship

Deadline: March 1, 2021

Amount 1 x $4,000 and 3 x $2,000.  Applicant must produce a three-minute video OR write a well-researched fact based 1000-word essay highlighting their 'food story' – the relationship between agriculture and the food they eat (see website for more info).  Must show proof of acceptance to a post-secondary institute (in an agriculture-related program) and letter of recommendation of teacher or agricultural industry or community leader. 

 

Saskatchewan Oddfellows and Rebekah Bursary

Deadline:  Please Check

If you have an average of at least 75% in grade 11 and 12, have a financial need, are active in the school and/or community, then you may be eligible for this $1,000 bursary.  

 

Saskatchewan Power/Energy Bursaries

Deadline:  Please Check with Post-Secondary Institute

42 industry and trades awards available with amounts varying from $1,000 - $3,000.  Students must be enrolled in the following electrical industry-related programs: business administration, management studies, electrical engineering, industrial systems engineering, electronic systems engineering, civil engineering, environmental systems engineering, power engineering technology, mechanical engineering technology, electrical engineering technology, civil engineering technology, instrumentation engineering technology, chemistry or business information systems.  Visit the website for more information.  YOU CANNOT apply on this webpage, you MUST consult the post-secondary institute you applied at.

 

Saskatchewan Youth Apprenticeship

Deadline:  Please Check  

$1,000.  Must complete the Youth Apprenticeship Program Passport and be enrolled in a post-secondary apprenticeship program.  To apply, pick up an application form at the SSO.

 

Union of National Employees Bursaries

Deadline: March 31 Each Year 

Various bursaries available ranging from $500 to $2,000.  You or your parent MUST be a member of the Union of National Employees.  Visit the website for criteria, as you may have to submit an essay. 

 

University of Regina Entrance Awards      

Deadline:  Varies - Please Check

$500 - $23,000 in prizes.  If you are a potential University of Regina student, check out the plethora of scholarships available.
